About ABU IT TECH LTD

Our Story

ABU IT TECH LTD was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the technology gap for businesses across Nigeria, particularly in Kano State. We recognized that many small and medium-sized enterprises struggled to access reliable, affordable, and comprehensive IT solutions that could help them compete in an increasingly digital marketplace.

Since our inception, we have grown from a small team of passionate technologists into a full-service IT and business solutions provider. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Today, we serve businesses across various industries, providing everything from managed IT support and custom software development to digital marketing and business registration services. Our holistic approach ensures that our clients have access to all the technology and business tools they need under one roof.
