What is Payroll & HR Management Software?

Our comprehensive Payroll & HR Management Software is designed to simplify and automate all aspects of employee management for Nigerian businesses. From tracking attendance and managing leave requests to processing payroll and generating tax reports, our solution handles it all efficiently and accurately.

Built with Nigerian labor laws and tax regulations in mind, our software ensures compliance while reducing administrative burden and eliminating manual errors in payroll processing.

Problems We Solve

Manual Payroll Errors

Eliminate calculation mistakes and ensure accurate salary payments every time

Time-Consuming Processes

Reduce hours spent on payroll processing to just minutes with automation

Compliance Issues

Stay compliant with Nigerian labor laws, tax regulations, and pension requirements

Poor Record Keeping

Maintain organized, searchable employee records and payroll history

Key Features & Benefits

  • Automated Payroll Processing: Calculate salaries, deductions, and taxes automatically
  • Employee Self-Service Portal: Allow staff to view payslips, request leave, and update information
  • Attendance & Time Tracking: Integrate with biometric systems or manual clock-in/out
  • Leave Management: Track vacation days, sick leave, and other time-off requests
  • Tax Compliance: Automatic PAYE calculations and tax report generation
  • Pension Management: Track and report pension contributions accurately
  • Multi-Branch Support: Manage employees across multiple locations from one system
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Generate detailed reports for management and regulatory bodies
  • Bank Integration: Direct salary payments to employee bank accounts
  • Mobile Access: Manage HR tasks on the go with mobile apps
