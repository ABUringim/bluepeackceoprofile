What is Accounting & Invoicing Software?

Our Accounting & Invoicing Software provides Nigerian businesses with a complete financial management solution. Track income and expenses, manage accounts receivable and payable, generate professional invoices, and gain real-time insights into your business's financial health.

Designed for ease of use, our software requires no accounting expertise while providing powerful features that accountants and financial professionals appreciate.

Problems We Solve

Cash Flow Challenges

Track outstanding invoices and get paid faster with automated reminders

Manual Bookkeeping

Eliminate spreadsheets and automate financial record-keeping

Tax Preparation Stress

Generate accurate financial reports for tax filing and audits

Lack of Financial Visibility

Get real-time insights into profitability and financial performance

Key Features & Benefits

  • Professional Invoicing: Create and send branded invoices in minutes
  • Expense Tracking: Record and categorize business expenses easily
  • Bank Reconciliation: Automatically match transactions with bank statements
  • Financial Reporting: Generate profit & loss, balance sheets, and cash flow reports
  • Multi-Currency Support: Handle transactions in Naira and foreign currencies
  • Payment Integration: Accept online payments directly through invoices
  • Inventory Management: Track stock levels and cost of goods sold
  • Client Portal: Allow customers to view and pay invoices online
  • Automated Reminders: Send payment reminders automatically
  • Tax Compliance: Calculate VAT and generate tax reports
